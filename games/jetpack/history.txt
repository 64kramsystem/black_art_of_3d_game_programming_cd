Changes in JETPACK
==================


Registered Version 1.1
----------------------
FEATURES:
o Now you can edit over 10,000 levels.  They are now called anything
  with the extension '.JET'
o When playing customized levels, you can choose 'SELECT' to pick each
  level to play, 'ORDER' to play them in the order they are on the disk,
  or 'RANDOM' to play random levels.
o Save game feature added.  ALT-S between levels to save.  Select
  'SAVEGAME' with F4 to load an old game.

BUGFIXES:
o Multi-player games locked up sometimes.  Now works correctly.
o New Sound Code, version 1.5.  Fixes a few lock-up problems.
o Minor high score bug fixed.
o Joysticks threshold more sensitive
