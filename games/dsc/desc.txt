Desert Storm Command, by MVP Software, gives you hot Gulf War action.  
It utilizes authentic video-capture animation, 256-color graphics, explosive digitized sound, and a sizzling original musical score add.  In combat mode you have to protect your troops as they move through the city by taking out the enemy.  Next, you'll have to protect the city from Scud missle attacks by using your Patriot missles. Target Scuds, helicopters, and passing fighter planes.  The registered version has two additional games.  
Desert Storm Command has all the action to make this the best Gulf War arcade game of them all!  
Strap on your helmet and let's go!
